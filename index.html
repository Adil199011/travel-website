About North Goa City Tour:



Gear up for an exciting North Goa City tour and immerse yourself in the serenity of the sparkling sea, the tranquillity of beaches, and incredible Goan architecture. Learn everything about the Portuguese and Indian cultures as the knowledgeable guide will narrate some interesting facts for you. Visit the old and well-known monuments of Goa and enjoy your time admiring their magnificent architecture.


About the Activity:



Make your way to Fort Aguada and explore one of the famous lighthouses of Goa, a 17th Century Portuguese fort. Head towards the St. Alex Church and admire the beauty of the beautiful pristine white church. Enjoy visiting famous and pristine beaches like Baga Beach, Vagator Beach, Anjuna Beach, etc. Reach the famous Shree Chauranginath Bhumika Panchayatan Mandir and offer your prayers along with your family & loved ones. Know about the history and interesting facts about the places you visit from an experienced guide during the tour and return back with your loved ones and some beautiful memories to cherish.
